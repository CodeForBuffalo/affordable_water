{% trans "What if I need additional help?" %}

You may qualify for additional Pathways to Affordable Water. These include

  • Low Income Senior Citizen Discount (must be 65 years of age)
  • Monthly Payment Plans
  • Extended Repayment Periods
  • Interest Charge Suspension
  • Monthly Billing Option
  • Leak Abatement Program

{% trans "Can I apply as a renter?" %}

{% trans "Yes! Though it may depend on who pays the water bill." %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} If you pay your own water bill, you may apply normally. If you do not pay your own water bill or if water is included in your rent, you might still be eligible for water assistance. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} It is possible for your landlord to receive the water discount on your behalf. However, this is not legal advice and we highly recommend reviewing the Tenant's Rights Guide prepared by the office of the New York State Attorney General. {% endblocktrans %}
