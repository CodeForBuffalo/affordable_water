{% trans "What if I need additional help?" %}

You may qualify for additional Pathways to Affordable Water. These include: • Low Income Senior Citizen Discount (must be 65 years of age) • Monthly Payment Plans • Extended Repayment Periods • Interest Charge Suspension • Monthly Billing Option • Leak Abatement Program

{% trans "Can I apply as a renter?" %}

{% blocktrans %} If you pay your own water bill, you may apply normally. If you do not pay your own water bill or if water is included in your rent, you may still be eligible for water assistance. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} If your landlord receives the discount on your behalf, you could deduct that amount from your rent. However, this is not legal advice and we highly recommend a written agreement with your landlord. {% endblocktrans %}
