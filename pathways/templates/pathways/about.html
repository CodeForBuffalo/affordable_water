{% blocktrans trimmed %}We help people receive financial assistance on their Buffalo Water bill any time, from any device.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Apply now" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} We believe every Buffalonian deserves access to affordable water service with ease, efficiency and dignity. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Our goal is to make water bills more affordable for residential customers in our City, by providing bill access assistance, water conservation measures, and help navigating provided resources. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Eligibility for financial assistance is based on household income relative to federal low-income guidelines. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Households are considered automatically eligible for the Residential Affordable Water Program if the household contains a member who receives Supplemental Security Income (SSI), Public Assistance, Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP sometimes referred to as Food Stamps), or Home Energy Assistance Program (HEAP) benefits. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Buffalo Water proudly works together with community-based organizations to help ensure that water, Earth's greatest resource, is accessible to everyone. {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% blocktrans trimmed %}A website that works for everyone{% endblocktrans %}

    {% blocktrans trimmed %}With GetWaterWiseBuffalo.org, residents can apply for water assistance from any mobile phone or computer with an internet connection.{% endblocktrans %}

  • {% blocktrans trimmed %}A quick application for assistance{% endblocktrans %}

    {% blocktrans trimmed %}On GetWaterWiseBuffalo.org, residents can complete an application for water assistance in about 10 minutes or less.{% endblocktrans %}

  • {% blocktrans trimmed %}A simple way to send verification documents{% endblocktrans %}

    {% blocktrans trimmed %}With GetWaterWiseBuffalo.org, residents can take photos and submit required documents right from their phone. No scanners or snail mail is required.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}GetWaterWiseBuffalo.org was created by Code for Buffalo through the 2019 Code for America Community Fellowship.{% endblocktrans %}
