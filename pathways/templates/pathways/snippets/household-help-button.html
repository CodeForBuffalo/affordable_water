Not sure who counts?

  • Roommates: Only include roommates if you share living expenses for utilities and rent.
  • Immigrants: Any immigrant can choose to opt-out and will not be asked about their immigration status. If you wish, you may include them in the total household size.
  • Separation/divorce: In cases of separation or divorce, only include the other person if they live with you. Include your children if they spend most of their time at your home. The same child cannot be included on more than one application.
  • Pregnant mothers: Do not include unborn children on this application.
